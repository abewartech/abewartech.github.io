Sistem Online Pelabuhan (SOP)

July 30, 2024 — #Laravel#Vue.js#Maritime#Port Management

Sistem Online Pelabuhan (SOP) is a sophisticated port management platform designed to streamline and optimize port operations. The system provides comprehensive tools for vessel tracking, cargo management, and port operations monitoring, ensuring efficient maritime logistics and port management.

Key Features

  • Real-time vessel tracking and monitoring
  • Port operations management
  • Cargo tracking and management
  • Berth scheduling and allocation
  • Document management system
  • Port statistics and analytics
  • Multi-user access control
  • Integration with maritime systems

Services Offered

  • Vessel Arrival/Departure Management
  • Cargo Operations Tracking
  • Port Facility Management
  • Berth Planning
  • Port Security Management
  • Environmental Monitoring
  • Port Performance Analytics
  • Document Processing
